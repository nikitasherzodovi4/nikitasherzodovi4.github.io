Booking cheapest plane tickets is not always the easiest task to do. However, if you are knowledgeable in this area, some flight tickets might cost you as much as train or bus, but will get you from point A to point B way faster and with better comfort.

Always search for flights in incognito or private browsing mode to see the lowest prices (shift+n in most modern browsers)

Try to avoid Saturdays and Sundays. Weekend flight are generally more expensive than working days flights.

If you know when and where you’re going, don’t wait to book Rarely ever do airline tickets get cheaper as your departure date approaches, especially if you need to fly on a certain date. Budget airlines typically offer low rates as a baseline price, and as these tickets sell, the remaining ones increase in cost. This is very typical in Europe and North America. If you know when and where you’re going, don’t wait on an unknown sale. More often than not, your biggest savings come from booking far ahead when you can.

It is not necessary to book the seats, unless you really want to enjoy the view. It will save you 3-5 $ depending on the airline company.
